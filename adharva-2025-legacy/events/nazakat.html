Fashion Show Event

Event Overview

Venue

Munshi Auditorium

Team Composition

â€¢ 1 team per school: 3 designers + 3 models

Theme & Preparation

  • Theme announced after inaugural ceremony.
  • Costume prep begins only after theme announcement.
  • Each team: 3 costumes + at least 1 relevant prop.
  • All materials (except charts, newspaper, safety pins) to be brought by participants.
  • Costumes must be worn over a black base layer.

Materials Provided

  • Charts, Newspaper, Safety Pins provided.
  • Other materials (scissors, accessories, etc.) must be brought by students.

Performance & Explanation

  • 4 minutes for ramp walk.
  • 3 minutes for designers to explain concept and outfits.
  • Explanation must be concise and relevant.

Music & Green Rooms

  • Soundtrack to be submitted during registration on fest day.
  • Green rooms allotted after inaugural ceremony.

Prohibited Items & Conduct

  • No flammable materials, hazardous props, sharp objects, glitter bombs, or smoke-producing items.
  • Respectful conduct required backstage and during performance.

Judging & Decisions

  • The decision of the judges is final and binding.
