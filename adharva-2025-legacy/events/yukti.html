Yukti (Quiz)

The Ultimate Interschool Quiz Challenge

Event Overview

General Info

  • The quiz will have a total of 4 rounds.
  • Each school may send up to 2 teams (2 participants per team).

Round 1: Written (Library)

  • 20 teams (2 per school), 30 minutes, 20 questions (15 main + 5 tiebreakers).
  • Answers on provided question paper; write names and school.
  • Bring your own pen (no pencils allowed), only pen and ID card permitted.
  • Cheating = immediate disqualification.
  • 10 teams eliminated based on marks.

Round 2: Oral (Library)

  • Each team: 5 questions, 20 seconds per question.
  • Correct answer: 2 points. No negative marking.
  • Wrong answer: passed ONCE to next team (10 seconds, 1 point if correct).
  • 5 teams qualify for next round.

Round 3: Buzzer (Munshi Auditorium)

  • 15 questions. Buzz in to answer (10 seconds).
  • Correct: 2 points. Wrong: -1 point. No passing.
  • No teams eliminated, points are accumulated.

Round 4: Picture (Munshi Auditorium)

  • 20 questions (15 × 2 points, 5 × 3 points).
  • Questions on screen, 15 seconds to answer.
  • Passed to next team if no/wrong answer (half points for passed questions).
  • No negative marking.
  • Each team will be asked 4 questions.

Winner Selection

  • Winners decided by total points from Rounds 3 & 4.
