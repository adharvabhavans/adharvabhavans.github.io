Naada Nirvana

Band Competition

Event Overview

Venue

Munshi Auditorium

Team Composition

  • 1 team per school
  • Minimum 5 participants (2 singers & 3 instrumentalists)
  • Maximum 9 participants (if more than 5, at least 3 singers required)

Equipment & Instruments

  • Only Drums, IEMs, and Mics will be provided.
  • No other equipment will be provided.
  • Participants must bring their own instruments and accessories (guitar pedals, cables, drumsticks, etc.).
  • Participants are responsible for their own instruments.

Performance Rules

  • All performances must be live (no lip-syncing or playback singing).
  • No political, classical, devotional, or inappropriate songs.
  • No pre-recorded MIDI files, rhythm machines, karaoke tracks, or drum pads.
  • Shoulder/regular synthesizers and USB-MIDI controllers are allowed (no pre-recorded MIDI loops).

Judging & Decisions

  • The decision of the judges is final and binding.
