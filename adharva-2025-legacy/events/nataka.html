Nataka

The Art of Spontaneous Theater

Event Overview

Team Composition

• Maximum 3 members per team

• One team per school

Time Structure

• Round 1: 30 minutes for plot development

• Round 2: 1 hour for script writing

• Round 3: 15 minutes preparation + 15 minutes performance

Performance Guidelines

• Props must be school-approved

• Basic stage lighting will be provided

• No pre-written scripts allowed

• All dialogue must be in English

Judging Criteria

• Creativity and originality

• Team coordination

• Acting skills

• Stage presence

• Time management

Important Notes

Rules & Regulations

• No controversial or inappropriate content

• Props must not damage school property

• Teams must report 15 minutes before their slot

• Time penalties for exceeding limits

Prizes

• First Place: Trophy + Certificate

• Second Place: Medal + Certificate

• Third Place: Medal + Certificate

• Best Actor: Special Recognition
