Natya Sutra

Group Dance Competition

Event Overview

Venue

Munshi Auditorium

Team Composition

• 1 team per school, max 8 participants

Time Limits

• 3 minutes for stage setup

• 7 minutes for performance

Props & Setup

  • Schools may bring their own props (must not damage property or obstruct event flow).
  • Props must be manageable and cleared off stage by participants immediately after performance.
  • Use of fire, water, sharp objects, powder, or hazardous materials is strictly prohibited.

Themes & Conduct

  • No controversial or sensitive themes (religion, politics, cultural stereotyping, etc.).
  • Costumes and choreography must be appropriate for a school audience.
  • Any vulgarity or obscenity (attire, lyrics, performance) = immediate disqualification.

Music & Lighting

  • Audio tracks must be submitted during registration on fest morning.
  • Bring a backup copy on a pen drive.
  • Lighting arrangements will remain standard for all teams.

Judging & Decisions

  • The decision of the judges and organizing committee is final and binding.
